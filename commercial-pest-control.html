Customized Pest Control Solutions For Your Business

As a business owner, you juggle your customers, employees, and your company's priorities every day. Sometimes these three interests conflict, and sometimes they converge. One way in which they converge is when you come up against a pest problem. A pest infestation in your business is bad for your customers, your employees, and your company. 

Some of the threats of having a pest infestation in a commercial setting include loss of customers, negative reviews, damage to your business's overall reputation, exposure to illnesses and safety risks for your employees and customers, loss of money in the form of costly repairs, damaged inventory or equipment, and other financial obligations. That's why it's so important to bring in the pest control professionals at White Knight Pest Control to have the problem addressed quickly, effectively, and discreetly.

To protect your top three priorities, you need a pest control plan that is not simply reactive, but one that looks ahead to anticipate and prevent potential problems before they can occur. With this proactive pest protection in place, you can ensure that your facility maintains a pest-free environment and that your business continues running smoothly without the worry of pests. At White Knight Pest Control, we'll come alongside you to develop, implement, and sustain a pest control program tailored to your business's needs.

Our Commercial Pest Control Treatments

When you choose to partner with White Knight Pest Control for your commercial pest control needs, you won't ever have to worry about receiving a one-size-fits-all solution to your pest problems. Right from the start, we'll customize our treatment plan to meet the unique needs of your specific business and industry.

We'll begin by sending one of our most experienced technicians to meet with you. This meeting allows you to address your concerns, show us the areas of your facility where you've noticed problems or pest activity, and ask questions. Your technician will also want to look around the property to identify entry points, harborage areas, and conducive conditions you may not have noticed. 

After compiling all the necessary notes, your technician will write up a proposal and submit it to you for review. This proposal will detail the current areas of concern and provide a comprehensive plan for eliminating current pest activity and preventing future occurrences. It will also include pricing information. If you choose to approve the plan, we will move forward with a treatment schedule.

Our treatment protocols will vary based on the specifics of your specific pest control plan. Typically both exterior and interior treatments are included in our commercial pest control services and they will work to target the specific pests that your business has been having trouble with. We treat a wide variety of pests and specialize in providing pest control solutions for several particular pests that are especially common in commercial settings including rodents and cockroaches.

Follow-up treatments occur on a regularly scheduled basis. Your recommended service schedule will be outlined in your proposal and customized to your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are recurring services necessary?

If your goal is to both rid your business of active pests and keep pests out in the future, then recurring services are very important. While our treatments are extremely effective at eliminating and preventing pests, they begin to break down over time. By providing on-going services to your business regularly, we maintain its protection and keep it pest-free.

What industries do you service?

We provide our pest control services to a wide range of industries including:

  • Nursing Homes, Hospitals, and Other Healthcare Providers

  • Schools

  • Homeowners Associations

  • Condos and Apartment Buildings

  • Warehouses

  • Churches

  • Camps and Cabins

  • Office Buildings

  • Storage Facilities

If you don't see your industry listed above, please contact us to see if we can help!

Are your services guaranteed?

When you choose White Knight Pest Control, you also receive our service guarantee. Consider it your business's pest insurance — you probably won't need it, but it's there if you do! If any problem arises between service visits, contact us, and we are happy to come out and take care of it for you, free of charge.

How does your scheduling work?

We understand that treating pest problems in a business setting can be difficult. You need discretion and the least disruptive service possible. That's why we will work with you to develop a service schedule that works for your business's particular needs.

Do your treatment methods comply with our industry standards?

Every industry operates under certain guidelines, standards, and laws. When we develop your pest control program, we'll address those standards to ensure we are complying with your industry's protocols.
